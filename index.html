COVID-19

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The disease was first identified in December 2019 in Wuhan, the capital of China's Hubei province, and has since spread globally, resulting in the ongoing 2019–20 coronavirus pandemic.

Let's fight Corona

Do's:

#1 Fight the Villain by Staying at Home
YOU can stop the villain from taking over the world if you stay at home! If you do plan to step out for essentials, make sure to keep at least 1 metre distance between yourself and others.
#2 Wash Your Hands
Every time you forget to wash your hands, the villain grows stronger. Remember to always wash your hands with soap and water for 20 seconds.
#3 Dab to Defeat the Villain
To prevent the villain from attacking other people, you need to make sure that you: Dab when you sneeze. Dab when you cough.

Don't:

#1 Don’t Touch Your Face
Create an anti-villain force field around your face by not touching your eyes, nose and mouth.
#2 Don’t Spread Rumours. Spread Facts
Shield yourself and your family from rumours and half-baked information. Stand up to “fake news” and challenge the villains army of informants. And remember to always bust myths!
